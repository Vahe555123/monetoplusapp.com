Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 01, 2026

1. Introduction

Moneto Plus is an informational application that provides users with general statistics, analytical forecasts, and informational reports based on user responses. The application does not provide financial services, does not issue loans, and does not act as a lender, broker, or financial intermediary.

All results shown in the application are for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial advice, guarantees, or loan offers.

2. Information We Collect

Moneto Plus collects only limited information voluntarily entered by the user, including:

• Name
• Desired loan amount
• Income amount

No contact details such as email address, phone number, or payment information are collected within the application.

3. How We Use Your Information

The entered information is used to generate statistical analysis and general forecasts regarding potential loan eligibility.

The user's name and desired loan amount are stored locally on the device to improve user experience. Income information is not stored locally.

Data required for analysis is transmitted securely to our server for processing and generation of statistical results. The results do not represent approvals, guarantees, or financial commitments.

4. Data Storage and Security

Locally stored data remains on the user’s device. Server-side data is used only for analytical processing and is protected using reasonable technical and organizational security measures.

While we take appropriate steps to protect information, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

5. Analytics

Moneto Plus uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics such as application interactions and performance metrics.

Firebase Analytics does not provide us with personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve application functionality and stability.

6. Third-Party Services and Report Requests

The application may offer users the option to contact us via WhatsApp to request a free extended informational report.

Communication via WhatsApp occurs outside the application and is initiated voluntarily by the user. Moneto Plus does not provide consultations, financial advice, or personalized recommendations through third-party communication platforms.

7. Your Rights

You may stop using the application at any time. Since no user accounts are created and no contact details are stored, data retention is limited to the functionality required for analysis and local application use.

Users located in Spain, Mexico, and Chile may have additional rights under applicable data protection laws.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the application. Continued use of Moneto Plus after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
